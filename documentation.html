Fox Valley Special Recreation Association
Incident Management System

IMS is an incident management web application developed for a local business in Aurora, Illinois, working with a team of four people in an agile scrum environment. The application was built utilizing React, Node.js, REST architecture, and SQL. I was responsible for the following:
  • Translating the customer's verbal and written needs into technical requirements
  • Led in writing the majority of the formal documentation, including the requirements specification, project proposal, and product backlog
  • Designed and created the majority of the frontend from scratch
  • Provided assistance with the backend and database.
